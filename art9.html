The veiled Godess (2020)

3D Remix

Description

I created this 3D design remix by downloading the veiled character and stylizing it in Adobe Dimensions. I wanted to create a veiled dark Goddess that portrays power and mystery as well as kindness. The character was initially white, I changed its color to black and added a smooth shiny texture to it. I added the white wings to create a contrast between the figure and the background.
