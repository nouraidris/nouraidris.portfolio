City Living(2016)

Poster

Description

This poster was a project for my honors in Computer Graphics. I wanted to make a poster for one of my favortite cities. I started off with a blank page in illustrator and created shapes and vectors to frame and create the layout.

I then added images of different parts of the city that I took myself. I used layer masks and clipping techniques to create the circular images on the bottom right. Lastly, I created the textures and textiles in Photoshop and Illustrator.
