Wallpaper(2020)

Illustration

Description

This illustration started with a small project of me painting “GRL PWR” on my jeans jacket. I’m a supporter of all women and the girl power movement. A photo of me wearing the jacket and posing with my fist raised to emphasize the word power on the jacket. I then used this photo to create an illustration. To create this piece, I changed the colors and duplicated the illustration multiple times in a new document to create a wallpaper background out of it. I use this piece as my logo because it represents me and what I stand for.
