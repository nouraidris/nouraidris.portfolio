Infect (2020)

Mixed media interactive installation

Description

IThis was one of the most exciting projects I have worked on. It was challenging but I got to work with multiple mediums. I picked out a plank of wood and carved out the edges in curves to create a more interesting shape. On the backside of the plank, I have attached an LED strip to spell the word Infect. In the front, I have drilled holes in equal spaces to have the light travel through the plank. On the drilled holes I screwed small fake plastic bulbs that acted as a prop for a real one when in reality it was lit by the led strip. To make the piece interactive, I installed a sensor on the top left that was programmed to set off the lights in a sequential pattern.
