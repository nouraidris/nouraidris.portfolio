ABOUT ME

I love everything art!

Welcome to my website! I am majoring in Digital Media Art and minoring in Photography. I have been practicing photography for 6 years now. I specialize in landscape, architectural, and portrait photography. I specifically aim to capture unique moments, to truly reveal the essence and beauty of the person or object in front of me. Alongside my photography, I use various applications such as photoshop, illustrator, and InDesign to further enhance my images. I am very passionate about creating aesthetically pleasing projects which have inclined my passion for web development and Design. This website encapsulates my work, from my very first Html website to my award-winning photographs.





Who am I?





My name is Noura Idirs. I'm Eritrean (East African) and I was born and raised in Saudi Arabia. I moved to the United States in 2012, currently living and residing in San Jose, California. I love expressing myself through my photography and artwork. Alot of my projects are cultural and socio-political.I would describe my art as being Digitally focused, mainly black and white and provoking at times. I enjoy discovering new Mediums and challenging my self.
