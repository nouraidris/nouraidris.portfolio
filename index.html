NO-RA IDRIS

GET TO KNOW ME

I love everything art!



Welcome to my website! My name is Noura Idris. I'm Eritrean (East African) and I was born and raised in Saudi Arabia. I moved to the United States in 2012, currently living and residing in San Jose, California.

I am a digital media artist and I specifically specialize in using mixed mediums to convey my message. As an immigrant, the topics I tackle in my work are deeply rooted in my personal experience. I enjoy using photography, creative coding and 3d rendering to express myself. The topics of my work are ever-changing as my skills and experiences develop. My passion for Digital Media started with photography. I have been practicing photography for six years now. Through editing my pictures and creating digital collages I have stumbled upon the world of Digital Media. A lot of my current projects are cultural and socio-political. I would describe my art as being Digitally focused and provoking at times. I use softwares such as Adobe suite, Python and JavaScript to make these works possible.
